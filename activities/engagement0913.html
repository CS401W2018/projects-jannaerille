What are special entity characters?

Any kind of special character that you want to show on your page. You can use a "<p>". You can search google for other codes you can use.

What is the difference between OL or UL Tags?

UL is an unordered list, the order doesn't matter. OL is an ordered list, where the order is ranked specifically.
