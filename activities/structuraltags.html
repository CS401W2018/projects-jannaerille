Engagement Questions:

What goes in the header?

   You could put your logo. But you usually put what you want the people to see first when they open your page.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

   The Nav contians the different links within your webpage. Sometimes, the Nav goes inside the header. But sometimes, it goes under the header.

What goes in Main?

    Where the main content of your page goes.

What goes in the Footer?

    Usually, we put our copyright. You could also put social media links.
