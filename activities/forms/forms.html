Engagement Questions:

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The "action" attribute in an HTML form tag specifies where the form data should be sent when the form is submitted.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The "method" attribute in an HTML form tag specifies how the form data should be sent to the server when the form is submitted. It could be GET or POST

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

When we do a GET method, all the information goes up to the URL, it is for reading information. POST is adding or changing information.
