Humba

Humba is a delectable Filipino dish that will tantalize your taste buds with its rich, savory flavors.1 This traditional braised pork recipe combines the tender richness of pork belly with a harmonious blend of soy sauce, vinegar, and a hint of sweetness, creating a depth of flavor that is both comforting and indulgent. The slow-cooked pork falls apart effortlessly, infused with the aromatic essence of garlic, onion, and spices, making each bite a culinary delight. Whether served at a family gathering or a special occasion, Humba is sure to leave you craving for more. Its unique flavor profile and tender texture make it a must-try for anyone looking to experience the best of Filipino cuisine.

Recipe at a glance:
Ingredients Quantity Unit of Measurement
Pork Belly 1 lbs.
Garlic 5 cloves
Vinegar 1/3 cup
Brown Sugar 1/4 cup
Soy Sauce 1/4 cup
water 1 cup
Bay leaf 1 leaf
Crushed Pepper To taste
Oil 1 tsp.
Salt To taste
