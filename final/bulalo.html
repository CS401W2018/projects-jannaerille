Bulalo

Bulalo is a hearty and comforting Filipino beef shank soup that warms the soul with its rich, flavorful broth and tender, fall-off-the-bone meat. This traditional dish is slow-cooked to perfection, infusing the beef with the deep flavors of onions, garlic, and peppercorns, while vegetables like cabbage and bok choy add a fresh and crunchy texture. The clear broth, enriched with the marrow of the beef shanks, is a savory delight that pairs perfectly with steamed rice 1. Whether you're looking for a comforting meal on a chilly day or a satisfying dish to share with family and friends, Bulalo is sure to hit the spot and leave you feeling full and content. Its comforting aroma and delicious taste make it a beloved staple in Filipino cuisine.

Recipe at a glance:
Ingredients Quantity Unit of Measurement
Beef Shank 2 lbs.
Cabbage 1/2 head
Bok Choy 1 bundle
Corn 2 cobs
Whole Peppercon 2 tbsp.
water 34 oz.
Green Onions 1/2 cup
Onion 1 bulb
Fish Sauce To taste
