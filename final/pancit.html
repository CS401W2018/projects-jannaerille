Pancit Canton

Pancit Canton is a mouthwatering Filipino stir-fried noodle dish that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Manila.1 This beloved comfort food, with roots tracing back to 16th-century Chinese traders, has evolved into a true Filipino classic. Imagine tender wheat noodles mingling with succulent pieces of pork, chicken, or shrimp, and a colorful medley of crisp vegetables, all bathed in a savory blend of soy sauce and oyster sauce. The noodles, symbolizing long life in Filipino culture, are often the centerpiece of birthday celebrations and festive gatherings.2 With its perfect balance of textures and flavors, Pancit Canton offers a delicious journey through Filipino history and culture in every satisfying bite.

Recipe at a glance:
Ingredients Quantity Unit of Measurement
Flour Stick Noodles 250 grams
Pork 4 oz
Chinese Sausage 1 piece
Onion 1 bulb
Garlic 1 teaspoon
Shrimp 8-10 pieces
Snap Peas 10-12 pieces
Carrots 3/4 cup
Cabbage (small) 1 head
Chicken broth 1 1/2 cups
Oyster Sauce 1 tbsp
Soy Sauce 3 tbsp
Water 3/4 cup
Oil 3 tbsp
Salt & Pepper To taste
