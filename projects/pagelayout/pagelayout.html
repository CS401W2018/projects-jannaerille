Meet my Friends

ALOHA! I would like to introduce you to my 5 friends-turned-family here in Hawaii. We call ourselves The 5 Idiots, after the famous movie, 3 Idiots. We all came to Hawaii from the Philippines last Winter 2024 and that's how we met. In our group, there's Clabs, Sage, AJ, Jadd and me!

We all work in the same department in the Polynesian Cultural Center-- the Guest Services Department. The girls are tour guides and the boys are canoe guides. We love spending time together, watching movies, eating out, driving around the island, and taking lots of pictures.
